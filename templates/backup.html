Fudicial Tag Positioning

Robot positioning using QR code-like fudicial tags.

March 2022 - April 2022
Image Processing, Robot Positioning, Robotics
In the HKUST Robotics Team, I helped develop a robot positioning system using [AprilTags](https://april.eecs.umich.edu/software/apriltag), which are QR code-like fudicial tags. By sticking AprilTags on the robot and using OpenCV and numpy to process camera footage, we could obtain the robot's coordinates relative to the game field.
